Pancakes

Description

You want to make a good, fast, and funny breakfast? Here's your guide to make them

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • Â½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted, or vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Toppings

Steps & Indications

  1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees; In a small bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together milk, butter, and egg. Whisk until moistened, but do not overmix.
  3. Heat a large skillet or griddle over medium. Rub the skillet with oiled paper towel.
  4. For each pancake, spoon 2 to 3 tablespoons of batter onto skilet.
  5. Cook until surface of pancakes have some bubbles and a few have burst, 1 to 2 minutes. Flip carefully with a thin spatula, and cook until browned on the underside, 1 to 2 minutes more.
  6. Serve warm, with desired toppings.
