Gnocchi

Description

What's better than to get home at night and being able to prepare a good plate of gnocchi?

Here's some instructions so you can make them too!

Ingredients

  • Salt
  • Mashed Potatoes
  • Pepper
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Oil

Steps & Indications

  1. Boil and mash the potatoes.
  2. Combine the ingredients, then knead into a ball
  3. Shape the dough into "snakes"
  4. Cut the snakes into pieces.
  5. Boil and drain the gnochhi

A tip and a fun fact

You can serve them whit virtually any sauce or salsa.

Gnocchi can be made two days ahead of time, or even be stored (freeze) up to six weeks.
